This was my college graduation project. We did it in groups of 4. My task in this project was face recognition system and socket communication parts.

To talk about the details of the project, it is an image processing-based system that can be used in states and armies. As you can imagine, engaging in close combat and being stuck in a residential area has always been a challenging task for our troops. However, we know that all states, especially the FBI and CIA, categorize wanted criminals with red, blue and gray lists. For this reason, it can be integrated on vehicles such as military vehicles and helicopters, especially drones, and can take action according to their position in the wanted list by scanning all human faces around from the moment it is started. In addition, by sending notifications to the soldier and his commander, who are in control, through a mobile application, thanks to the authorization system. We have designed a project that can provide location detection or target destruction with a single button.

As I mentioned above, I undertook and successfully completed the socket connection required for the person-based facial recognition we targeted in this project and instant sharing of the captured target with mobile applications.

I used python while writing this script. Haarcascade, which offers ready-made models with opencv, was used on the image processing side. Websocket was also used in the socket part
